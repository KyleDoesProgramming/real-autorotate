# Privacy Policy for Real AutoRotate

**Last Updated:** August 2025  

## Introduction

Real AutoRotate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application "Real AutoRotate" (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **App Selection**: Your choice of applications for which you want auto-rotate enabled
- **Service Preferences**: Your settings for the auto-rotate service

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality
- **App Usage Data**: Information about which applications are currently in the foreground (required for core functionality)
- **Service Status**: Whether the auto-rotate service is enabled or disabled

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
- **Location Data**: We do not collect or track your location
- **Browsing History**: We do not collect your web browsing history
- **Contact Information**: We do not access your contacts or address book
- **Messages or Communications**: We do not read your messages, emails, or other communications

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

1. **Core App Functionality**: To enable/disable auto-rotate settings based on your selected applications
2. **Service Management**: To monitor which application is currently in the foreground
3. **App Performance**: To ensure the app functions properly and efficiently
4. **User Experience**: To remember your app selections and preferences

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- All your data is stored locally on your device
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- Your information remains on your device and is not shared with third parties

### Data Security
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- Your data is stored securely on your device using Android's built-in security features
- We do not have access to your stored data

## Permissions and Their Purpose

### Required Permissions

| Permission | Purpose | Data Access |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| `PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS` | Detect which app is currently in the foreground | Only app package names, no personal data |
| `WRITE_SETTINGS` | Toggle system auto-rotate settings | System settings only |

### Permission Details
- **Usage Stats Permission**: This permission allows us to detect which application is currently running in the foreground. We only use this information to determine when to enable/disable auto-rotate settings. We do not collect or store this information.
- **Write Settings Permission**: This permission allows us to change the system's auto-rotate setting. We only modify this specific setting and do not access any other system settings.

## Third-Party Services

### No Third-Party Data Collection
- We do not use analytics services
- We do not use advertising networks
- We do not integrate with social media platforms
- We do not share data with third-party services

### External Libraries
We use the following open-source libraries for app functionality:
- **Foreground App Checker**: Used to detect which app is currently in the foreground
- **Android Toggle**: Used for the toggle button UI component

These libraries are used solely for their intended functionality and do not collect or transmit any user data.

## Data Retention

### Local Data
- Your app selections are stored locally on your device
- This data persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- We do not maintain any copies of your data on external servers

### Service Data
- Service status and preferences are stored locally
- No historical data is maintained beyond what's necessary for current functionality

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- You can view and modify your app selections at any time within the app
- You can enable or disable the service at any time
- You can uninstall the app to remove all stored data

### Data Deletion
- Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
- You can also clear app data through your device's settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your information is:
- **Legitimate Interest**: To provide the core functionality of the app
- **Consent**: When you grant the required permissions

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Equal service and price

Since we do not collect personal information or sell data, most of these rights are not applicable to our app.

## Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving your information, we will:
1. Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
2. Provide details about what information was compromised
3. Outline steps we're taking to address the breach
4. Provide guidance on steps you can take to protect yourself
